About Masaka Social Centre

The Masaka Social Centre is a renowned community space located on Bwala Hill in Masaka, Uganda.
Known for hosting significant cultural, educational, and social events.
it plays a key role in the community's daily life.

With its central location, the centre has become a hub for both local gatherings and events of national importance.

Accessibility and Transportation

Getting to Masaka Social Centre is straightforward.
It is accessible from Kampala, with transportation options that include buses, taxis, and private cars.
The journey from Kampala typically takes about three hours.

Upon arrival in Masaka, visitors can take a boda boda from the city center to the centre,
which is located close to Hill Road Primary School.

Notable Events

The centre frequently hosts events that attract both local and international participants.
highlighting the venue's role in promoting digital a educational development in the region.
